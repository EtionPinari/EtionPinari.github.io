Who am I?

My name is Etion Pinari, a BSc graduate in Politecnico di Milano, in the field of Computer Engineering, currently studying in the masters level (MSc).
Proficient in Italian (B2 certified CELI), English (C1 certified TOEFL) and Albanian (mother language) I consider myself a person who you can speak freely to.

I have had the pleasure of becoming experienced in many programming languages such as those of web design, data sciences, OOP, logical and electronical modelling.
My interests in the work field consist of videogame design and software engineering. In my free time I tend to read books about managment and design, work out, practice playing the piano, but mostly study for the exams.

This is a small project so that I can get used to HTML, CSS and Javascript coding.
